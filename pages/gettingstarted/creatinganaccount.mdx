## Setting up

- Open the NCCRM application [Link to the Application](https://earlywarning.gomindz.gm/auth).
- Enter your username and password provided to you by your system administrator.
- Click the "Login" button.
- If your role is "Super Admin," you will be directed to the Super Admin Dashboard
- If your role is "Admin," you will be directed to the Admin Dashboard
- If your role is "Analyst," you will be directed to the Analyst Dashboard
